About

Torzsi   

Ádám "torzsi" Torzsás (born May 17, 2002) is a Hungarian professional Counter-Strike: Global Offensive player who plays for MOUZ as the AWPer.He was unable to attend any Valve-sponsored events due to a VAC ban which occurred on November 10, 2016; following Counter-Strike's "New RMR Eligibility Guidelines" blog post published in April 2021, torzsi's ban expired on the same date in 2021.

JDC   

Jon "JDC" de Castro (born April 7, 2000) is a German-Portuguese professional Counter-Strike: Global Offensive player for MOUZ. He often takes on the role of the voice of the team, speaking for the team in interviews and such. He is very calm and collected and keeps analyzing his game to improve. JDC joined mouz NXT in June 2021.


Frozen   

David "frozen" Čerňanský (born July 18, 2002) is a Slovak professional Counter-Strike: Global Offensive player for MOUZ.David was very young, he started at the age of 13, which means he is the youngest player.He joined mousesports Main CS:GO team in March 2019.

Dexter   

The Australian Ingame leader actually started his career in Call of Duty, however he soon realized that he prefers CS:GO as it’s more strategic. The bravest step in his professional esports career was probably leaving his home scene in Australia and going to Hamburg, Germany to play in the European CS:GO scene. Chris is a calm and collected individual with an interest in food and cultures. He started playing for mousesports in February 2021.


Bymas   

Bymas has proven his skills by winning FPL in 2019 at the young age of 15 and many have recognized his potential. He is talented, skilled and a hard-working player who keeps surprising the scene. He started being a pro player at the age of 17 which is daring, admirable and takes a lot of work and dedication to also deal with school at the same time. He has played for mousesports since August 2020.

Sycrone (The Coach)   

The Head Coach of the team is very grounded and shows attention to detail when it comes to the analysis of the game and improving with every match. He has been with a couple of teams and gained a lot of experience when coaching the MOUZ NXT Team. From 2022 he started coaching the Main CS:GO Team with the promotion to the Head Coach. He joined MOUZ in June 2021.
